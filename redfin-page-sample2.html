About this home

Discover the comfort and flexibility of this traditional–contemporary bi-level home offering over 3,000 sqft of living space on a beautiful 1.3-acre lot. Situated just off the main road, the property enjoys excellent natural light, charming curb appeal, and expansive front and rear yards with trees lining the back for privacy. The upper main level welcomes you with high cathedral ceilings that enhance the bright and open feel of the primary living area. The inviting living room centers around a charming fireplace, creating the perfect focal point for gatherings or cozy evenings at home. This level features 3 spacious bedrooms and 2 full bathrooms highlighted by a large primary suite with high ceilings, dual sinks, and a separate tub and shower. The thoughtful layout offers exceptional versatility, with two distinct living rooms—perfect for separating everyday relaxation from entertaining. A bonus room adding more options and can easily serve as a 5th bedroom, home office, or creative workspace. On the lower level, you’ll find the fourth bedroom, the bonus room, and a full bathroom, making it ideal for guests, extended family, or a private workspace. A large recreational area on this level provides endless possibilities for a game room, home gym, theatre, or family lounge. Outdoors, the wide-open 1.3-acre lot provides plenty of usable space for outdoor activities, gardening, and future ideas, with a large storage shed offering convenience and extra storage. With replacement of the rear deck, a few modern upgrades and personal touches, this home has the potential to become a true gem—spacious, adaptable, and full of opportunity. This home is selling AS IS. Make it your own, bring your vision, and unlock all the possibilities this property has to offer.
