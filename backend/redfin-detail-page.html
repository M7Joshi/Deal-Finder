About this home

PRICE REDUCED! Sweeping unobstructed Columbia River views from this well-maintained 3 bed, 2.5 bath rambler with walkout basement on 4.65 acres. In 2,500 sq ft of home space you have room to breathe! Upgrades incl metal roof, dbl-pane vinyl windows & sliders, radiant heat, propane fireplace & stove. Enclosed breezeway is hot tub ready. 384SF deck plus lower concrete patio make it ideal for entertaining. 2-car garage, plus separate huge 30x40 shop has 220-amp service. Property partially fenced for livestock, no CCRs—just peace, privacy, & wide-open views. Located minutes from Seaton’s Grove Boat Launch, Downriver Trail, and local favorites like Spring Canyon, Banks Lake & Steamboat Rock. Live where you love to play!
